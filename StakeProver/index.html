Stake Prover

This Dapp allows you to submit a hash of a text to the blockchain. The amount Ether you had in you account will be recorded. You can also optionally burn Ether when making a submission (this will be recorded). This could be used on forums to link all of your submissions to your Ethereum account.

Another potential use of this Dapp is to prove that you came up with some idea at a certain point in time. Just submit the hash of the description and it will be timestamped.

To verify a piece of text, enter into a box below and click "Get Info". (You are not sending a transaction in this case.)
